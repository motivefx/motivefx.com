Privacy Policy

Last updated June 6, 2026

This policy explains how MotiveFX LLC (“motiveFx,” “we”) handles information across motivefx.com and the ScreenSet Mac app. The short version: ScreenSet keeps your data on your own Mac, and we don’t sell or track you.

ScreenSet runs on your device

ScreenSet does not collect, transmit, or store your personal data. Your saved layouts and monitor setups live in a local database on your Mac and never leave it. ScreenSet uses macOS Accessibility permission for a single purpose — to read and move your windows on your own computer. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no account.

Unlocking Premium

When you unlock ScreenSet Premium, the app sends your license key and a randomly generated device identifier to our licensing provider once, to confirm the purchase. We use this only to validate your license. We never receive or store your payment details.

Purchases

Payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy, our Merchant of Record. Lemon Squeezy collects and handles your billing information under their own privacy policy. motiveFx never sees or stores your card details.

Contacting us through the site

If you reach out through the contact form, we collect what you choose to send — your name, company, phone, email, and message — and use it only to respond to you. We use Cloudflare Turnstile to keep out spam, and our host keeps standard server logs (basic request data) for security and reliability.

What we don’t do

We don’t sell your data, run advertising trackers, or share your information beyond what’s needed to answer you or process a purchase you’ve made.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete any information you’ve sent us. Email hello@motivefx.com and we’ll take care of it.

Changes

If we update this policy, we’ll revise the date above. Material changes will be noted on this page.
